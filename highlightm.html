OnePlus Nord CE 5G

OnePlus Nord CE is a 5G-ready mid-range smartphone loaded with a Snapdragon 750G processor with 6GB of RAM + 128GB of UFS 2.1 storage, a 64MP triple rear camera, a 6.4-inch AMOLED punch-hole display, and is backed by a 4,500mAh battery supported by a fast 30T warp charger. Read the complete specs details of the OnePlus Nord CE 5G and check whether it matches your preferences.

OnePlus Nord CE 5G Display and Camera

OnePlus Nord CE 5G includes a 6.4-inch fluid AMOLED display with a punch-hole. It comes with a resolution of 1080x2400px with a pixel density of 410ppi. Moreover, the display refreshes at 90Hz and offers nearly 85% of the screen-to-body ratio. And, you get an in-display fingerprint sensor for security unlocks. As for the optics, the Nord CE 5G carries a 64MP triple rear camera having dual-LED flash. Its primary 64MP camera with an aperture of f/1.7 is placed next to an 8MP ultra-wide sensor, and a 2MP macro camera with autofocus. For assisting selfies and video calls, you get a 16MP punch-hole selfie camera. Also, useful features such as Nightscape, UltraShot HDR, Portrait, Panorama, Pro Mode, Smart Scene Recognition, etc are present.

OnePlus Nord CE 5G Performance and Battery

OnePlus Nord CE 5G is driven by a Snapdragon 750G 2.2GHz octa-core processor. Needless to say, SD 750G SoC is a compatible SoC for mid-range gaming smartphones. Also, it comes with 6GB of RAM and Adreno 619 GPU for performance boosts. User-friendly sensors like Accelerometer, Electronic Compass, Gyroscope, Ambient Light Sensor, Proximity Sensor, etc are present. As far as the battery capacity is concerned, the smartphone gets juice from a 4,500mAh non-removable Li-Po battery and it gets charged from a fast 30T warp charger out of the box. Judging upon the battery capacity, a full day of power backup is expected on normal usage.

OnePlus Nord CE 5G Storage and Connectivity

OnePlus Nord CE 5G comes with 128GB of inbuilt UFS 2.1 storage that boosts its performance. Also, the storage capacity is ample and can be used to store games, apps, photos, videos, music, etc. However, it doesn’t have any extra card slots. The phone’s connectivity options are 4G, 5G (in-selected markets), VoLTE with Dual standby, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.1, NFC, a 3.5mm headphone jack, and a Type-C port for charging and OTG. For online navigation, you can use GPS with A-GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, BDS, and NavIC.
